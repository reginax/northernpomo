GRAMMAR

Phonology

There are 10 vowels total because [a, e, i, o, u] are phonetically distinct when lengthened. There are 29 consonants which is categorized by manner of articulation as follows: stops [b, d, p, t, t̪, k, ʔ, ph, th, t̪h, kh, p’, t’, t̪’, k’], affricates [c, c’, ch, ch’, ts, ts’], fricatives [s, sh, h], nasals [m, n], laterals [l], and glides [w, y].

Morphology

Northern Pomo is agglutinative as seen with phrases where morphemes retain their original meaning even when joined; for instance "let’s sleep" is simamiti-ya or "sleep-let’s".
