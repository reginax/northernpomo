Vocabulary List

The table below contains the a list view of the words at the QR Codes page on Northern Pomo Language Tools. The voice recordings were made by linguist Catherine O'Connor of Edna Campbell Guerrero and Elenor Stevenson Gonzales, two late Northern Pomo native speakers. These recordings are now archived at the Grace Hudson Museum.


Click the Northern Pomo word to hear the corresponding word pronounciation. Click on a column header to sort by that column.
